Baalbeck - The City of the Sun

Welcome to Baalbeck!

Baalbeck is a stunning historical city in Lebanon, famous for its exceptionally well-preserved Roman ruins. Known as Heliopolis during Roman times, Baalbeck boasts some of the largest and most impressive Roman temples ever built, making it a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Lebanon's most treasured landmarks.
